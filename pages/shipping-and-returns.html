Shipping

Shipping is always free within the US! All products are shipped through USPS and shipping takes 3-5 days for in-stock items.

For international orders, please email us at info@quincyapparel.com. Currently we are offering international orders for final sale only.

Returns and Exchanges

Unworn, unwashed, undamaged, or defective items will be accepted for full refund in the form of the original payment within 14 days of the date you receive the product.

RETURN SHIPPING

Return shipping is free and easy! A pre-paid USPS return label is included with every order. Please allow 10-12 business days for us to receive and process your return. You will receive a confirmation email when we have received the package.

REFUNDS

Once we have received your package, we will refund your account. The refund will appear on your credit card within a few days from when you receive the confirmation email.

EXCHANGES

Items can only be returned for refund. If you need a different size, color or style, you can place a new order online, and send in your return for a refund.
